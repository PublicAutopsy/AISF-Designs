Major Info

ADV equipment and software are professional quality. State of the art computers, scanners, color and B&W printers as well as digital cameras. We also use the latest technology in terms of software and try to make updates on regular basis. Students have access to facilities and gear almost every day and hour of the week. We currently work with the FD and FMM programs in developing collateral pieces for the annual fashion show. We are also working on establishing a partnership with the DFVP and Audio departments in order to include TV commercials and Radio Commercials.
