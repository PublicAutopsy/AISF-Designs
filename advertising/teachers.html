Gary Acord, an ad veteran of 30 years, Gary Acord was a member of the original Activision team at J. Walter Thompson (JWT), working on some of the early Atari titles and later becoming a partner in his own agency handling assignments from Intuit, Stormfront Studios and Rocket Science video games. These days he has graduated from player to coach as lead faculty for the advertising coach as lead faculty for the advertising department, where his responsibilities include development of current curriculum and graduate portfolio classes. Gary currently serves on the Board of The Greater San Francisco Ad Club and the Steering Committee of the Creative Educators Forum at the annual Promax BDA Conference.

Harvey Bailey

415.865.0198 ext. ####

Harvey Bailey is an honors graduate of the University of Michigan, with major credit in psychology, anthropology, and sociology, and a certificate in journalism. He has been the Visiting Advertising Professional at Michigan State University, and a guest lecturer at University of Michigan and Oakland University. Harvey has been Vice President- Creative for Campbell-Ewald and V.P. of Creative Strategy for Publicis, both in Detroit. In Chicago, he was V.P. - Group Creative Director at Foote, Cone and Belding; Creative Director at Euro; and Executive V.P. and Director of Creative Services at Interpublic Group agencies. In San Francisco, he headed up his own ad agency, Left Brain.

Amy Kweskin

Amy Kweskin is a full time faculty in Fashion Marketing & Management, Advertising and Culinary Departments. In 2010 she was awarded the Faculty Trailblazer Award and in 2011 she participated in the national Advertising Curriculum Committee. She has presented training programs for the National Arts Marketing Project and earned a Masters in Arts Administration from Golden Gate University and Bachelors degrees in Cinema/Photography and English from Ithaca College.
